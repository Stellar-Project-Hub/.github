# Stellar Project Hub

**Empowering the Stellar Ecosystem through High-Velocity Open Source Contribution.**

Welcome to the **Stellar Project Hub**. We are an organization dedicated to building modular, scalable infrastructure and DeFi tools for the Stellar network.

---

## 🌊 Drips Wave Participation
We are proud participants in the **Stellar Drips Wave** program. Our goal is to provide a world-class experience for contributors by maintaining:
- **Modular Repositories:** Easy-to-navigate, well-structured codebases.
- **Bite-Sized Tasks:** Clearly defined, manageable issues for every project.
- **Rapid Reviews:** Fast feedback loops for all Pull Requests to keep you moving.

## Our Focus Areas
- **DeFi Infrastructure:** Soroban smart contracts and liquidity tools.
- **Developer Experience (DX):** Scaffolding and CLI tools for Stellar builders.
- **Inclusion:** Bridging Stellar assets to real-world utility.

## 🫱🏻‍🫲🏾 How to Contribute
1. **Sign Up:** Join the community at [drips.network/app](https://www.drips.network/app).
2. **Find Us:** Navigate to **Wave > Stellar > Orgs/Repos** and locate **Stellar Project Hub**.
3. **Browse Issues:** Explore available tasks in any of our repositories and apply.
4. **Deliver:** Once approved, get to work and deliver results efficiently and effectively.
5. **Submit & Earn:** Open a Pull Request; once reviewed and approved, your points will be awarded.
